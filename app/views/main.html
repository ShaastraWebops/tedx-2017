Avnish Pandya
"Active participant in the advocacy of of industrial Hemp"

Avnish is the Co-Founder and Director of R&D at Bombay Hemp Company(BOHECO),a hybrid social enterprise transforming agriculture in developing economies. His core function at BOHECO involves engagement with various elements of the Hemp ecosystem including the likes of local farmers and industries to collaborate and create functional and sustainable hemp based products. Having always been deeply affected by the growing number of farmer issues and the apathetic state of the environment, he has shaped BOHECO's vision of educating the Indian society about the crop and its versatile benefits.

Dr. Soumya Swaminathan
"Re-energizing tuberculosis research in India"

Dr Soumya is the Director General of the Indian Council for Medical Research and Secretary, Department of Health Research- Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India. She is part of the TB Zero City Project which aims to create "Islands of elimination" working with local governments, institutions and grassroots associations

Arun Vinayak
"Co-designed India's first smart electric scooter,Ather-S340"

Arun is the Chief Product Officer and Founding Partner of Ather Energy. Arun built his first functional car in his 12th grade with second hand parts and scrap. His experience then triggered many automotive endeavors such as leading the FSAE team of IIT Madras,racing at the Hockenheimring F1 circuit,Germany.

Dr Smita Premchander
"Driving a large poverty alleviation movement with emphasis on women empowerment"

Dr Smita is the Founder-Director of Sampark and visiting faculty at the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad. Sampark is a Bangalore based NGO then enables poor women to gain direct control and improve their lives through educational interventions.

What is TEDx?



In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxIITMadras, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxIITMadras event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
